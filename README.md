# Stardew Valley - Living Roots

## Agroecological Mod for Stardew Valley

"Living Roots" is a mod (expansion pack) for Stardew Valley that aims to transform the game's farming experience by introducing the principles of agroecology. Moving away from intensive monoculture, this mod invites players to build resilient, self-sustaining, and socially just farms, reconnecting with the land and the community.

## Mod Philosophy

Inspired by agroecology as a science, practice, and social movement, "Living Roots" proposes an alternative to the conventional agricultural model, focusing on soil health, biodiversity, autonomy, and social justice. The goal is to create a food system that is environmentally sustainable, economically viable, and socially just within the Stardew Valley universe.

## Key Features (Overview)

### Core Concept: The Soil Health Bar
Every tillable plot on your farm now has a new property: Soil Health (visible when using a hoe or hovering the mouse, ranging from 0% to 100%). This metric directly influences plant growth, harvest quality, and pest resistance.

*   **Poor Soil (0-30%):** Slow growth, high chance of pests, lower quality produce (no stars).
*   **Medium Soil (31-70%):** Similar to the base game.
*   **Living Soil (71-100%):** Faster growth, chance of extra harvests, higher quality (silver/gold/iridium), and unlocks new interactions.

**What DESTROYS Soil Health:**
*   Leaving soil tilled and bare (without cover or plants).
*   Monoculture.
*   Using chemical fertilizers (new "Joja Fertilizer" items, which provide an initial boost but cost 5% soil health).

**What BUILDS Soil Health:**
*   Composting and organic fertilizer application.
*   Crop Rotation.
*   Polyculture / Companion Planting.
*   Green Manure.
*   Soil Cover (Mulch).
*   Animal Management.

### Game Progression

*   **Early Game (Year 1 - Learning the Fundamentals):** Introduction to the new NPC Elena, composting, soil cover, simple companion planting ("The Three Sisters"), heirloom seeds, and rainwater harvesting.
*   **Mid Game (Year 2-3 - Building the System):** Crop rotation, green manure, integrated animal management (mobile coop), beneficial insects and beekeeping, living pharmacy, and bio-construction (adobe shed, green roof).
*   **End Game (Year 4+ - Sovereignty and Community):** Integrated Crop-Livestock-Forestry (ICLF), greywater recycling, short supply chains (Stardew Fair), Community Supported Agriculture (CSA), and the Collective Kitchen.

## Installation

1.  **Install SMAPI:** If you don't have it already, download and install the [Stardew Modding API (SMAPI)](https://smapi.io/) for your operating system.
2.  **Download the Mod:** Download the latest version of "Living Roots" from the releases page (or Nexus Mods, when available).
3.  **Extract:** Unzip the mod file into your Stardew Valley `Mods` folder (usually located in `Stardew Valley/Mods`).
4.  **Play!** Launch the game through SMAPI.

## Compatibility

*   Requires SMAPI 4.0.0 or higher.
*   Compatibility with other mods will be tested and documented as development progresses.
